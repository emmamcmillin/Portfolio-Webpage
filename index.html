About Me

My name is Emma Mcmillin. I am 19 years old. I was born in Edmonton in the fall and moved to Calgary when I was 4. I have a younger sister named Celestine who I love and a tabby cat named Tigger.

I like to read books during my free time. I have always loved to read ever since I was young.

I mostly read fantasy novels. I'm not a fan of non-fiction books.

My Favourite Foods
